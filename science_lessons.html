Lesson Description

Theory Note - Lesson 1: Introduction to Physics

Physics is the study of matter, energy, and the fundamental forces that shape our universe. In Lesson 1, we will cover the foundational concepts of physics:

  1. Motion and Forces: Understanding the basics of motion and the forces that influence it.
  2. Energy and Work: Exploring the concepts of energy and its relationship with work.
  3. Waves and Optics: Investigating the nature of waves and the principles of optics.
  4. Electricity and Magnetism: Delving into the world of electric and magnetic fields.
  5. Thermodynamics: Examining the principles governing heat, temperature, and energy transfer.
  6. Modern Physics: Introducing the concepts of relativity and quantum mechanics.

By the end of Lesson 1, you will have a solid understanding of these fundamental physics concepts, paving the way for more advanced topics in subsequent lessons.
