Lesson Description

Theory Note - Lesson 1: Introduction to Business

Business Studies introduces students to the dynamic world of commerce. In Lesson 1, we will cover the following key topics:

  1. Business Basics: Understanding the nature and scope of businesses, their objectives, and various types of business entities.
  2. Entrepreneurship: Exploring the entrepreneurial spirit, the role of entrepreneurs in the business world, and the process of starting a business.
  3. Business Ethics: Examining ethical considerations in business, corporate social responsibility, and the impact of ethical decisions on stakeholders.
  4. Marketing Principles: Introducing marketing concepts, market research, and the marketing mix (product, price, place, promotion).
  5. Financial Management: Basics of financial planning, budgeting, and financial statements in business.
  6. Human Resource Management: Understanding HR functions, recruitment, training, and employee relations.

By the end of Lesson 1, students will gain foundational knowledge crucial for a comprehensive understanding of business studies.
